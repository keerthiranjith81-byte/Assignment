Biryani Recipe.

Ingredients :

  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • 500g Chicken
  • sliced 2 large Onions
  • chopped 2 Tomatoes
  • 1 cup Curd
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Whole spices (Cinnamon, Cloves, Cardamom, Bay leaf)
  • 2 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tbsp Biryani Masala
  • Fresh Coriander & Mint Leaves
  • Oil & Ghee
  • Salt - as needed
  • Water - as required

Steps To Prepare :

  1. First wash and soak the rice for 30 mins.
  2. In a pan,Heat oil and add the whole spices.
  3. Then add sliced onion and fry it until it turns golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and saute for a minute.
  5. Add chipped tomatoes and then add mint and coriander leaves.
  6. After that add chicken & curd.Add chill powder and Biryani masala
  7. Add 4 cups of water and cook until the chicken half done.
  8. Add the soaked rice.Cover tightly and cook on low flame (dum) for 15-20 minutes.
  9. Gently mix and serve hot with raita.

Enjoy your delicious homemade Biryani with Onion raita
